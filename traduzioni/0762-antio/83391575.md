# Antitrogus_ciliatus

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_ciliatus)*

***Antitrogus ciliatus*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (north-eastern New South Wales).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 21–23 mm (0.83–0.91 in). They are uniformly bright reddish-brown.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded in late November.
