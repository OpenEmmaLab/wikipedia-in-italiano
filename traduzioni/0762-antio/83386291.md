# Antitrogus_tumidifrons

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_tumidifrons)*

***Antitrogus tumidifrons*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (New South Wales).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 17–20 mm (0.67–0.79 in). The head, pronotum and legs are bright reddish brown, while the elytra and ventral surface are yellowish brown.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded in October.
