# Antipino

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antipino)*

**Antipino** (Russian: Антипино) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative center of Antipinsky Selsoviet, Togulsky District, Altai Krai, Russia. The population was 1,412 as of 2013. There are 12 streets.

## Geography

Antipino is located on the Chumysh River, 37 km south of Togul (the district's administrative centre) by road. Kolochkovo is the nearest rural locality.
