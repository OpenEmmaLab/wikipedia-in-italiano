# Antitrogus_villosus

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_villosus)*

***Antitrogus villosus*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (central-western Victoria).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 15.5–16.5 mm (0.61–0.65 in). The head, pronotum (mottled with dark brown), scutellum, pygidium and venter are brown, while the elytra are dark brown to more reddish brown near the lateral margins.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded in November.
