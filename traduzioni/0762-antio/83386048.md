# Antitrogus_nox

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_nox)*

***Antitrogus nox*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (mid-coastal region of New South Wales).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 21 mm (0.83 in). The head, pronotum and scutellum are black, while the elytra, abdomen and legs are very dark brown. The antennae and palpi are brown.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded in March.
