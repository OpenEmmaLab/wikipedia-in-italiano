# Antitrogus_robertsi

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_robertsi)*

***Antitrogus robertsi*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (northern New South Wales).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 19–22 mm (0.75–0.87 in). The body and legs are fairly uniformly bright reddish brown, but sometimes dark brown, with a sericeous bloom. The antennal lamellae are pale yellowish brown, while the remainder of the antennae and palpi are brown.
