# Antiquities_(film)

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antiquities_%28film%29)*

***Antiquities*** is a 2018 American comedy film directed by Daniel Campbell and starring Andrew J. West, Michaela Watkins, Michael Gladis, Ashley Greene and Mary Steenburgen.

## Cast

* Andrew J. West as Walt
* Ashley Greene as Ellie
* Michaela Watkins as Dolores Jr.
* Roger Scott as Blundale
* Graham Gordy as Jimmy Lee
* Melanie Haynes as Aunt Patty
* Natalie Canerday as Estate Sale Customer
* Michael Gladis as Delaney
* Ingrid Haas as Sarah
* Jennifer Pierce Mathus as Crazy Girls Waitress
* David Bazzel as Delivery Man
* Mary Steenburgen as Dr. Margot
* Troy Hogan as Dewey Ray

## Reception

The film has a 100% rating on Rotten Tomatoes, based on five reviews with an average rating of 7.3/10.
