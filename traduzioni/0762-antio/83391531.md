# Antitrogus_batesii

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_batesii)*

***Antitrogus batesii***, the **blacksoil scarab**, is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (New South Wales, Queensland).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 14–17 mm (0.55–0.67 in). The head, pronotum and scutellum are black to dark reddish-brown, while the elytra are reddish-brown or yellowish-brown, the propygidium yellowish-brown, the pygidium brown to black and the ventral surface of the thorax and legs dark brown to black.
