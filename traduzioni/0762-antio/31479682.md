# Antje_Weisgerber

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antje_Weisgerber)*

**Antje Weisgerber** (17 April 1922 – 29 September 2004) was a German film and television actress, and the wife of actor Horst Caspar.
