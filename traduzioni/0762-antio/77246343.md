# Antiquus_(name)

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antiquus_%28name%29)*

**Antiquus** is a surname. Notable people with the surname include:

* Bernardus Compostellanus Antiquus (fl. 13th century), Spanish canonist
* Johannes Antiquus (1702–1750), Dutch painter
* Lucius Pullaienus Gargilius Antiquus (fl. 2nd century), Roman senator
* Quintus Coredius Gallus Gargilius Antiquus (fl. 2nd century), Roman senator
