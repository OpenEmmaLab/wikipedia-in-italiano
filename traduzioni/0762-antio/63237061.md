# Antipino,_Gryazovetsky_District,_Vologda_Oblast

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antipino%2C_Gryazovetsky_District%2C_Vologda_Oblast)*

**Antipino** (Russian: Антипино) is a rural locality (a village) in Vokhtozhskoye Rural Settlement, Gryazovetsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 5 as of 2002.

## Geography

Antipino is located 62 km (39 mi) southeast of Gryazovets (the district's administrative centre) by road. Vokhtoga is the nearest rural locality.
