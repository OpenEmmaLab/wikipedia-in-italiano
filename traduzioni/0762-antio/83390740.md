# Antitrogus_mussoni

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_mussoni)*

***Antitrogus mussoni***, the **canegrub**, is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (northern New South Wales, southern Queensland).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 20–25 mm (0.79–0.98 in). They are bright reddish brown, with the frons and the disc of the pronotum sometimes darker.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded from December to January.
