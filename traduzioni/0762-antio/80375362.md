# Anto_Sekongo

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anto_Sekongo)*

**Yirigue Foungognan Sekongo** (born 13 June 2004) is a Malien professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Ligue 2 club Dunkerque.

## Club career

Sekongo joined Reims on 6 July 2023.

Sekongo joined Dunkerque on July 16, 2024, signing a 2-year contract.
