# Antitrogus_politus

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_politus)*

***Antitrogus politus*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (southern Queensland).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 21 mm (0.83 in). The head and pronotum are pale reddish brown, while the elytra and abdomen are yellowish brown.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded from November to December.
