# Antitrogus_circulifrons

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_circulifrons)*

***Antitrogus circulifrons*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (northern New South Wales, southern Queensland).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 19 mm (0.75 in). They are light reddish brown, with the antennal club pale yellowish brown.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded from November to December.
