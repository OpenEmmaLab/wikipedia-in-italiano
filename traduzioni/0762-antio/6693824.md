# Äntligen_på_väg_(Lotta_Engbergs_album)

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%84ntligen_p%C3%A5_v%C3%A4g_%28Lotta_Engbergs_album%29)*

***Äntligen på väg*** is a 1996 studio album from Swedish singer Lotta Engbergs. The album peaked at #13 on the Swedish album chart.
