# Antlers_Municipal_Airport

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antlers_Municipal_Airport)*

**Antlers Municipal Airport** (IATA: **ATE**, FAA LID: **80F**) is a public airport one mile (1.6 km) southwest of the central business district of Antlers, a city in Pushmataha County, Oklahoma, United States. It is owned by the City of Antlers.

## Facilities and aircraft

Antlers Municipal Airport covers an area of 97 acres (39 ha) which contains one asphalt paved runway (17/35) measuring 4,001 ft × 60 ft (1,220 m × 18 m). For the 12-month period ending August 7, 2018, the airport had 2,300 aircraft operations, all of which were general aviation.
