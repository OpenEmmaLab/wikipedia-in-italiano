# Antithesis_(Gypsy_album)

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antithesis_%28Gypsy_album%29)*

***Antithesis*** is the third album by the American rock band Gypsy, their first on the RCA Victor label.
