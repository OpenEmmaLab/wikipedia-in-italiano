# Antipina,_Krasnovishersky_District,_Perm_Krai

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antipina%2C_Krasnovishersky_District%2C_Perm_Krai)*

**Antipina** (Russian: Антипина) is a rural locality (a village) in Krasnovishersky District, Perm Krai, Russia. The population was 192 as of 2010. There are 5 streets.

## Geography

Antipina is located 57 km southeast of Krasnovishersk (the district's administrative centre) by road. Parshakova is the nearest rural locality.
