# Antitrogus_setifer

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antitrogus_setifer)*

***Antitrogus setifer*** is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in Australia (central coast of Queensland).

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 16 mm (0.63 in). The head, pronotum and scutellum are bright reddish yellow, while the elytra and abdomen are yellowish brown and the antennal lamellae are pale yellowish brown.

## Life history

Adults have been recorded in October.
