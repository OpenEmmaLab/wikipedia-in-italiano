# Getinghonung

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Getinghonung)*

**Getinghonung** (English: Wasp's honey) is a 1974 album by Dutch-Swedish singer-songwriter Cornelis Vreeswijk.
