# Get a Life (film)

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Get_a_Life_%28film%29)*

***Get a Life*** (Portuguese: *Ganhar a Vida*) is a 2001 Portuguese drama film directed by João Canijo. It was screened in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.
