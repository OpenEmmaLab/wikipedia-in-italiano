# GetResponse

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GetResponse)*

**GetResponse** is an email marketing platform for managing marketing and sales campaigns via email.

GetResponse was founded by Simon Grabowski in 1998 and is headquartered in Gdańsk, Poland.

GetResponse acquired Recostream, an AI product recommendations startup in 2022.

## Awards

In 2024 and 2023, GetResponse won the MarTech Breakthrough Awards in the fields of email marketing.
