# Getov

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Getov)*

**Getov** (Bulgarian: Гетов) is a Bulgarian masculine surname, its feminine counterpart is **Getova**. It may refer to

* Plamen Getov (born 1959), Bulgarian football player
* Preslav Getov (born 1965), Bulgarian football player
* Venera Getova (born 1980), Bulgarian discus thrower
