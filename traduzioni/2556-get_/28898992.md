# Get_Up_(Richie_Kotzen_album)

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Get_Up_%28Richie_Kotzen_album%29)*

***Get Up*** is the thirteenth album by guitarist/vocalist Richie Kotzen. It was released on September 25, 2004.
