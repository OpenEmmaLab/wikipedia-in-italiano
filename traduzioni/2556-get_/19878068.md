# Get Off This

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Get_Off_This)*

"**Get Off This**" is a song by American rock band Cracker. It appears on their 1993 album, *Kerosene Hat*. "Get Off This" reached number 6 on the *Billboard* Modern Rock Tracks chart and number 18 on the *Billboard* Album Rock Tracks chart in 1994.

## Release and reception

"Get Off This" was the second single released from the album, *Kerosene Hat*. Jason Gross from *Rock and Roll Globe* said it was "one of the band’s best songs" and that it "could be seen not only as a poke at hippies and idealists but also the 'fans' who are disappointed that Cracker ain’t Camper Van Beethoven."

## Music video

The music video for "Get Off This" features animation including rotoscoping, and was directed by Carlos Grasso.
