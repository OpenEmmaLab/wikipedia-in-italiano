# Stiliyan_Makarski

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stiliyan_Makarski)*

**Stiliyan Makarski** (Bulgarian: Стилиян Макарски; born 18 March 1985) is a Bulgarian badminton player. In 2008, he won the Banuinvest International tournament in mixed doubles event.
