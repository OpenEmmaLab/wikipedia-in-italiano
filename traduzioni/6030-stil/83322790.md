# Stilbotrochalus

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stilbotrochalus)*

***Stilbotrochalus*** is a genus of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is monotypic, being represented by the single species, ***Stilbotrochalus nitens***, which is found in Tanzania.

## Description

Adults reach a length of about 6.75 mm (0.266 in). They are entirely brownish-red, strongly glossy, and neither tomentose nor hairy. The pronotum is strongly narrowed anteriorly, superiorly almost densely punctate. The elytra are punctate-striate, with the striae somewhat impressed and the interstriae sparsely punctate. The pygidium is weakly convex, partly slightly, but very faintly, foveolate and sparsely, but rather strongly punctate.
