# Still_Shinin

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Still_Shinin)*

***Still Shinin*** is the debut studio album by American rapper and singer 4Batz, released on September 12, 2025, through BuVision. The album features guest appearances from Flo, Leon Thomas, Maxo Kream and Zillionaire Doe; Anycia also makes a cameo appearance.

## Release and promotion

On April 4, 2025, 4Batz released "Act X: N Da Morning" as the lead single from *Still Shinin*. On August 23, 2025, he announced the albuum and released "Act XIII: My Lil Shootah". It was released through BuVision on September 12, 2025, and was accompnied by a music video for "Act XI: She Ain't No Angel". 4Batz is due to embark on the I'm Still Shinin Tour from October 28 to November 28, 2025.

## Critical reception

According to *The Source*'s Shawn Grant, *Still Shinin* "cements [4Batz's] reputation as one of music’s rising voices" and "[pushes] his artistry to new heights." Malcolm Trap of *Rap-Up* praised Teddy Walton's production for making the album come together like a "cinematic masterpiece". Sharmaine Johnson of *BET* thought that 4Batz was "carrying the torch for R&B the way folks have been begging for" and that the album had "no skips".
