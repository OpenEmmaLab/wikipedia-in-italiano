# Stilfontein_(House_of_Assembly_of_South_Africa_constituency)

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stilfontein_%28House_of_Assembly_of_South_Africa_constituency%29)*

**Stilfontein** was a constituency in the Transvaal Province of South Africa, which existed from 1910 to 1994. Named for the town of Stilfontein, throughout its existence it elected one member to the House of Assembly.
