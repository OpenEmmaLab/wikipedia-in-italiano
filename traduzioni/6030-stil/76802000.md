# Stillwell_Run

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stillwell_Run)*

**Stillwell Run** is a 1.46 mi (2.35 km) long first-order tributary to the Occoquan River in Fairfax County, Virginia. This is the only stream of this name in the United States.

## Course

Stillwell Run rises on the Maple Branch divide and then flows southwesterly to join the Occoquan River about 2.5 miles south-southwest of Makleys Corners.

## Watershed

Stillwell Run drains 0.67 square miles (1.7 km2) of area, receives about 43.4 in/year of precipitation, and is about 84.36% forested.
