# Stiliyan_Tisovski

*[Voce originale su Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stiliyan_Tisovski)*

**Stiliyan Emilov Tisovski** (Bulgarian: Стилиян Емилов Тисовски; born 1 October 2003) is a Bulgarian footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Bulgarian Second League club Hebar Pazardzhik.

## Career

Tisovski spent his youth years at the academy of Hebar Pazardzhik from 2015 to 2020. On 16 May 2020, he made his debut for the first team against Litex Lovech. In February 2023, he signed his first professional contract with the team.
